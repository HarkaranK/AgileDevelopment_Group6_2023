About US

At Smart Quest, we are a dedicated team of five Computer Information Technology (CIT) students from the
British Columbia Institute of Technology (BCIT). Our passion for learning and technological innovation has
driven us to develop this webapp as part of our ACIT 2911 course project. We believe in the power of
interactive and engaging learning experiences, and our goal is to empower users by providing them
with a platform that fosters curiosity and growth. Through our combined expertise and commitment,
we strive to create a cutting-edge quiz platform that makes learning enjoyable and accessible for everyone.
